Ring Circuit Architectural Advantages

"In a ring circuit, the current divides roughly equally between the two legs. The effective resistance from the source to any socket is halved compared to a radial circuit of the same total cable length. This reduces voltage drop and allows 2.5mm² cable to safely carry 32A, whereas a radial circuit would require 4mm² or 6mm² cable."

Series-Parallel Circuit Behavior

"When the series bulb (C) is removed, the entire circuit goes dark demonstrating that a single break in the series portion stops current everywhere. However, removing one parallel bulb (A) leaves the other parallel bulb (B) still lit, showing the redundancy of parallel branches."
